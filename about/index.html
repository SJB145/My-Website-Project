A little bit about me

I’ve made my home base in Phoenix, Arizona, with my small growing family. Before coding, I was a registered dietitian, providing interventions for patients with nutrition problems. While I enjoyed working in this field, there was always something missing. I found Skillcrush and began my coding journey. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
